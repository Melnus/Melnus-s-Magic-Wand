# Melnus's Magic Wands

<img src="./melnus_magicwands_behavior_pack/pack_icon.png" width="60%">

A Minecraft Bedrock Add-on that introduces a powerful elemental magic system alongside a complete alchemical progression tree. 

Unleash devastating spells with seven ancient magic books and transmute base cobblestone into rare materials, life forms, and advanced potion reagents.

---

## Overview

Melnus's Magic Wands blends spellcasting with skyblock-style alchemy. 

Instead of searching the world for rare resources or equipment, players utilize a deterministic crafting economy to transform raw blocks into powerful reagents, eventually unlocking the ultimate magical casting tools: the **Magic Books**.

The core philosophy remains:
> Harness the elements, transmute the world.

---

## Core Features

### 🪄 1. Seven Elemental Magic Books
Craft seven distinct magic books (wands) using experience bottles and elemental materials. Each book shoots unique projectiles with specific combat utilities:

*   **🔥 Fire Wand (炎の魔導書)**: Fires rapid-fire small fireballs that ignite targets.
*   **🌀 Gravity Wand (重力の魔導書)**: Launches homing projectiles that inflict Levitation (180s).
*   **❄️ Ice Wand (氷の魔導書)**: Hurls freezing snowballs that apply extreme Slowness.
*   **💨 Wind Wand (風の魔導書)**: Fires concentrated airbursts that trigger kinetic explosions and knockback.
*   **💧 Water Wand (水の魔導書)**: Launches high-velocity water spits dealing solid impact damage.
*   **💀 Dark Wand (闇の魔導書)**: Shoots wither skulls that inflict total Blindness.
*   **✨ Light Wand (光の魔導書)**: Launches lightning-fast arrows dealing massive damage (18).

---

### ⚗️ 2. Comprehensive Alchemical Engine
A fully-realized crafting progression tree that allows you to manufacture essential blocks, rare items, and mob spawns:
- **Nether & End Blocks**: Craft Netherrack, End Stone, End Rods, and Obsidian.
- **Froglights & Eggs**: Generate Frog Spawn Eggs and all three variants of Froglights.
- **Alchemical Charcoal**: A late-game "reward" recipe allowing you to bypass slow smelting furnaces once you reach the Diamond tier.

---

### 🧪 3. Potion Reagent Transmutation
Never search for rare structures or hunt elusive mobs to brew your potions. Complete your brewing arsenal purely through alchemical recipes:
- **Pumpkin & Melon**: Transmute potato + copper into Pumpkin, then evolve Pumpkin + moss into Melon.
- **Underground / Rare Drops**: Synthesize Pufferfish, Brown Mushrooms, Rabbit's Feet, and Dragon's Breath.

## Example Recipes

### Magic Books (Wands)
- Book + Soul Sand + Experience Bottle → Dark Wand ×16
- Book + Clay Ball + Experience Bottle → Ice Wand ×16
- Book + Gold Ingot + Experience Bottle → Light Wand ×16

### Alchemy & Materials
- Diamond + Experience Bottle → Charcoal ×2 (Furnace-free mass production)
- Potato + Copper Ingot + Experience Bottle → Pumpkin ×1
- Pumpkin + Moss Block + Experience Bottle → Melon Block ×1
- Cod + Spider Eye + Experience Bottle → Pufferfish ×1
- Glass Bottle + Obsidian + Experience Bottle → Dragon's Breath ×1

## Design Philosophy

**Deterministic Power**  
No RNG or exploration barriers. Progress through logical crafting tiers, obtain the ultimate diamond catalysts, and unlock infinite elemental magic.

**Combat meets Utility**  
Your magic books aren't just for damage—they are tactical tools designed to control, freeze, lift, and blind your foes.
